About Flour & Crust

Welcome to Flour & Crust!

Established in 2016, Flour & Crust is a family-run artisan bakery located in the heart of Cape Town. We are passionate about crafting delicious breads, pastries, and cakes using traditional techniques and the finest local ingredients.

Our Values

  • Quality: Every product is made fresh daily with care.
  • Community: We support local farmers and suppliers.
  • Creativity: Our bakers love experimenting with new recipes and flavors.
  • Sustainability: We use eco-friendly packaging and minimize food waste.

Meet Our Team

Our skilled bakers and friendly staff are dedicated to making your visit memorable. Whether you're picking up your morning loaf or ordering a custom cake, we're here to help!

Our Journey

Sarah Johnson started baking from her home kitchen, selling bread to neighbors and local cafés. Her sourdough starter "Lucy" was born this year and continues to be the heart of our bread-making process.

After years of growing demand, we opened our first location on Baker Street in Cape Town's Artisan Quarter. The community response was overwhelming, with queues around the block on opening day!

Master pastry chef Mike Chen joined our team, bringing his French training and innovative dessert creations. Our menu expanded to include croissants, éclairs, and seasonal specialties.

Due to popular demand, we opened our downtown and mall locations, making our artisan breads and pastries accessible to more of Cape Town's bread lovers.

We continue to innovate while staying true to our artisan roots. Our commitment to quality, community, and sustainability guides everything we do as we look toward the future.
